    title: A free, weekly home for Buffalo founders.
    body: >-
      Buffalo Open Coffee Club is a free, weekly networking event for Buffalo's small business
      and entrepreneurial community. Held every Tuesday morning from 7:30–9:00 AM in the lobby of
      [Seneca One Tower](https://senecaonebuffalo.com/), BOCC creates space for business owners,
      aspiring founders, and students to connect, collaborate, and grow.


      The format is simple: open networking followed by a community "Gives and Asks" session, where
      attendees share what they need help with — or offer help to others. It's a high-energy,
      no-frills, impact-driven meetup that welcomes first-timers and veteran entrepreneurs alike.
