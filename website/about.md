    label: Our story
    title: Born on Chippewa, reborn at Seneca One.
    body: >-
      Back in 2010, a small group (a packed morning was 10 people) of startup-interested folks
      used to gather for coffee on Tuesdays at Spot on Chippewa. It was the accidental founding of
      Buffalo's startup community. Before the pandemic,
      [Jack Greco](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jacklgreco/) and
      [Clark Dever](https://www.linkedin.com/in/clarkdever/) rebooted that concept and blew it up
      to 150 people a week.


      This post-COVID incarnation of the group, organized by
      [Zack Glick](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zglick/), is not exactly that group — but it is an
      attempt to gather the startup community in a chill spot to talk with other members of the
      community, to encourage chasing the impossible, and to be there for each other.
